  • Quaid-e-Azam

Quaid-e-Azam is a national hero of Pakistan.
He was born on 25 December 1876 in Karachi.
His real name was Muhammad Ali Jinnah.
He is the founder of Pakistan.
He demanded a separate homeland for Muslims of subcontinent.
He became first governor general of Pakistan.
He died on 11 September 1948,his tomb is in Karachi.
